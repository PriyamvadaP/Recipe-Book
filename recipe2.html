Paneer Tikka Masala

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer (Indian cottage cheese), cut into cubes
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon tikka masala spice mix
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, tikka masala spice mix, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt.
  2. Add paneer cubes to the marinade and coat them well. Allow it to marinate for at least 30 minutes in the refrigerator.
  3. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add finely chopped onions and saut√© until they become translucent.
  4. Add the tomato puree and cook until the oil starts to separate from the mixture.
  5. Reduce the heat and add cream, stirring continuously.
  6. Now, add the marinated paneer along with the marinade. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring gently.
  7. Sprinkle garam masala and mix well. Simmer for a few more minutes until the gravy thickens.
  8. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.
