Kerala Fish Curry

Ingredients

  • 500g fish (preferably kingfish or mackerel), cleaned and cut into pieces
  • 1 cup grated coconut
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 1 cup tamarind extract (thick pulp)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil

Instructions

  1. Marinate the fish pieces with turmeric powder, salt, and a little tamarind extract. Let it sit for 15 minutes.
  2. In a blender, combine grated coconut, green chilies, and fenugreek seeds. Blend to a fine paste using a little water.
  3. Heat coconut oil in a pan and add sliced onions. Sauté until they turn translucent.
  4. Add minced garlic and curry leaves. Sauté for a couple of minutes until aromatic.
  5. Add red chili powder and the coconut-green chili-fenugreek paste. Cook until the oil separates from the mixture.
  6. Add the marinated fish and tamarind extract. Simmer on low heat until the fish is cooked and the curry thickens.
  7. Adjust salt and tamarind extract according to your taste.
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice.
